# Demo Script

## Opening
"This is an AI receptionist designed for local trade businesses."

## Demo Flow

1. Customer starts chat from website.
2. AI answers service questions.
3. AI qualifies the lead.
4. Customer provides contact information.
5. Appointment request is created.
6. Business owner sees the lead in dashboard.

## Pitch

"Instead of losing customers after hours or while crews are busy, businesses can capture and qualify every opportunity."
